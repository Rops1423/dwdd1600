Overview

If you have used social media at all over the last few weeks them you have see all the news about the Apple Vision Pro.

Everybody is talking about the Apple Vision Pro, it impossible to avoid all this talk. Another topic on social media right now is comparing the Apple Vision Pro to the Meta Quest 3.

People are asking is the Apple Vision Pro really worth the $3.5k price, especially when the Meta Quest 3 only cost $500. This article will tell you the differences, the pros and cons of each device, and let you choose whats better.

What does XR mean?

What does AR mean?
Augmented Reality, an enhance, interactive version of the real world.
What does MR mean?
MR stands for Mixed Reality, a reality where virtual and real objects co-exist and interact in real time.
What does VR mean?
VR stands for Virtual Reality, a completely faked reality.
What does XR mean?
XR means Extended Reality, is a catch-all term referring to AR, MR, VR.

What is "Spacial Computing"?

Apple calls the Vision Pro a “spacial computing” device, but that does just mean a VR/MR headset. Just like what the Meta Quest 3 is.
